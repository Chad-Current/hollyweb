  • We've been training dogs for over 60 years.

  • Classes are open to all breeds of dogs 8 Weeks of age or older.
  • There are 2 classes each week to choose from, each are 1 1/2 Hours in length
  • One flat fee to train, for the lifetime of the dog
  • Complete companion dog obedience, service dog, and therapy dog curriculum geared to both Pet owner and competitor
  • Our curriculum includes heeling on leash, sit, stay, down, stand, come, and all off leash commands
  • Socialization exercises include: dog to dog, people to dog, and distraction resistance.
  • Two trainers are at each class to help you individually- before, during, and after each training session, as well as available 7 days a week by phone, text, or message.
  • CGC (Canine Good Citizen testing), the advanced title, and trick dog AKC titles are offered every 3 months.
